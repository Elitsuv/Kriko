Kriko AI

An open-source AI opponent for hand cricket, adapting to your moves in real time.

Get Started

Features

  • Gesture Recognition: Detects 1-6 finger gestures using machine learning.
  • Adaptive AI: Learns your playstyle to keep matches challenging.
  • Real-Time Play: Fast, lag-free responses for smooth gameplay.

How to Use

Play hand cricket against Kriko AI with these simple steps:

  • Hold up 1-6 fingers to make your move.
  • Kriko analyzes and responds instantly.
  • Outsmart the AI to score and win!
